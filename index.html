About me

I am an undergraduate student at the Lanzhou University in Lanzhou, China, majoring in Microelectronics and will receive my B.Eng. degree in July 2024. Recently my research has focused on signal processing and control, and I am willing to pursue a Master's Degree and PhD.

Outside of work, I engage in photographing and traveling. I greatly enjoy these kinds of activities and if you have the same preferences, feel free to contact me.

What i'm doing

I have always tried to explore new research and projects actively, mainly in signal processing and control. I have also taken various courses in physics, computing and mathematics and have had the privilege of working as a research assistant in a lab and have presented papers at conferences.

  • Machine Learning in Control

    Current and planned research interests during the Master's degree.

    • My current research interest is to achieve efficient and adaptive control systems through the application of machine learning methods to traditional control theory. There are already two relevant papers to be published. In addition, the courses I took during my undergraduate studies have provided the perfect support for my research in this area.

    I have recently started to expand my research area towards readout electronics and particle physics. My other research projects include work on optical systems and image recognition. To learn more about these topics, please click on the Resume tab at the top of this page for a detailed description of my research experience.
